### Jede - Every   (Adjective)

can also mean everybody when talking about people. and i think you almost never actually use the "en" form
"ich arbeite **jeden** tag" - "i work **every** day"

### Der/Die/Das

* Jede (fem)
* Jede (neut)
* Jeden (plural)
#### accusative:
* Jeden (masc)
