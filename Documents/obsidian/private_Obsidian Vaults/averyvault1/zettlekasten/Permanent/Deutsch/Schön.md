### Schön - Pretty/Beautiful   (Adjective)

can also mean "fine" or "great" or "nice" when referring to specific objects. not to be confused with [[Schon]]
"Du bist sehr **schön** <3" - "You are very pretty <3"

### Der/Die/Das
#### nominative:
* Schöne (masc)
* Schöne (fem)
* Schöne (neut)
* Schönen (plural)
#### accusative:
* Schönen (masc)


### Ein/e/n
#### nominative:
* Schöner (masc)
* Schöne (fem)
* Schönes (neut)
* Schönen (plural)
#### accusative:
* Schönen (masc)
