### Süß - Sweet   (Adjective/Adverb)

can also mean sweetie, and can also be used as an adverb
"Du bist sehr **süß** <3" - "You are very **sweet** <3"

* Süße (neut)
* Süßen (plural)
#### accusative:
* Süßen (masc)
