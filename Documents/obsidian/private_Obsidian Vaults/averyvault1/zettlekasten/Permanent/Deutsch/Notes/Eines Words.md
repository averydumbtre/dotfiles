Memorization Phrase! Men (der) and Children (Das) are trouble... so their word ending are different when using ein! (Women Are Flawless And Perfect Always)

* **Der** - neu**e** - Mann
* **Ein** - neu**er** - Mann

* **Die** - neu**e** - Frau
* **Eine** - neu**e** - Frau

* **Das** - neu**e** - Kind
* **Ein** - neu**en** - Kind

* **Die** (plural) - neu**en** - Bücher
* **Viele** - neu**en** - Bücher
